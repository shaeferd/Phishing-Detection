SOUND BYTES

*Something sure looks phishy
By Clay Ruth, Contributing Writer

Phishing scams are increasingly clever, but it still came as a
surprise to one security professional to receive an e-mail from a
leading security vendor requesting detailed information through an
insecure channel
 Though the questionnaire was legitimate, it
highlights the need for organizations across the industry to consider
what types of information they request -- and how -- before
enterprising phishers use this technique to really take us all to the
cleaners
 Find out how one expert was nearly fooled into offering up
information on his company network:
